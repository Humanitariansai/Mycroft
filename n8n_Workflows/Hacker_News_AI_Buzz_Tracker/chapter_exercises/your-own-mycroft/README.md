# Investment Research Desk (Your Own Mycroft)

This is a personal investment research desk. It gathers evidence for and against a holding, organizes the claims behind a thesis, and leaves every buy, hold, or sell decision to me. The assistant structures and flags, it does not decide and it does not place trades. The first holding under review is META. Each thesis is audited claim by claim, and a claim is treated as verified only after I open the source and confirm it myself.

## Contents

- CANNOT-KNOW.md: the charter of what no model and no market chatter can tell me.
- theses/: claim audits, one file per ticker. Currently META.
- evidence/: source material tied to claims, such as filing lines and data series.
- signals/: options and market signal notes, tested as hypotheses rather than followed.
- book/: notes and longer analysis.
