# META Research Effort Plan

This plan decides where my next research session on META should go. It builds on the verified claim audit in theses/META.md and on my charter in CANNOT-KNOW.md. The factual base, the full year 2025 revenue, cash flow, capital expenditures, and the Reality Labs loss, is already verified, so the open work is the forward looking thesis drivers and the weighting of the risks.

## Decision-moving, research first

| Item | What would settle it | Effort | If it resolved against me, would it change my view? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Does the heavy AI and capital spending actually convert into earnings growth rather than just higher costs | Track operating margin and free cash flow over the next two to four quarters against capital expenditures, and watch whether revenue growth holds as depreciation from that spending starts to hit the income statement | Medium, recurring | Yes |
| Is the improvement in ad performance real and actually driven by AI | The quarterly average price per ad and ad impressions trend in the next 10-Q filings, plus management commentary tying gains to AI ranking and recommendations | Low to medium | Maybe |
| Is a forward price to earnings ratio around 17 reasonable for the growth, or already pricing the AI payoff | Compare the forward ratio to expected earnings growth, to peers, and to META's own five year range | Low | Maybe|
| How large and how likely is a regulatory or legal impact | Read the 10-K Legal Proceedings and Risk Factors, and follow the European Union Digital Markets Act and the antitrust matters for any fine or remedy that would change how ads work | Medium | Maybe yes, but only if a remedy changes the advertising model itself. |
| Is the Reality Labs loss capped or still widening | Management guidance on the 2026 Reality Labs loss and the segment trend, and whether there is any stated ceiling | Low | Maybe. A widening loss with no cap would weigh on the case. |
| Are the new revenue opportunities producing any disclosed revenue yet | Future segment and revenue disclosures and earnings call commentary on AI products and business messaging | Medium | No|
